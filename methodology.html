Methodology

Sample Size

30 participants aged 18–40 years.

Inclusion Criteria

  • Healthy adults with no gait impairments.
  • No prior musculoskeletal injuries.

Exclusion Criteria

  • Neurological disorders affecting motor control.
  • Cardiovascular abnormalities.

Instrumentation

VR headset, thermal imaging camera, motion analysis system, EMG sensors.
