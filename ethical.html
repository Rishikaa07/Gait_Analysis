Ethical Considerations

Maintaining Ethical Integrity

This study strictly adheres to ethical guidelines for biomedical and neuromuscular research. All participants will be treated with respect, and their rights and safety will be prioritized.

  • Informed Consent: All participants will provide written consent before participation.
  • Confidentiality: Data will be anonymized and stored securely.
  • Right to Withdraw: Participants may withdraw from the study at any stage without penalty.
  • Risk Minimization: Safety protocols will be followed to prevent harm or discomfort.
  • Approval: Ethical clearance will be obtained from the Institutional Review Board (IRB).
  • Transparency: Results will be reported honestly without data manipulation.
