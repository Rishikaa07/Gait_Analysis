Research Objectives

  • Use VR and thermal imaging to analyze biomechanical adaptations in reverse walking.
  • Investigate neuromuscular responses in thigh and calf muscles.
  • Study changes in gait parameters like step length, cadence, and walking speed.
  • Analyze variations in joint angles during constrained gait.
  • Assess associated cardiovascular activity during reverse walking.
